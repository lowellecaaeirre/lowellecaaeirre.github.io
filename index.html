Bookstore | Personalized Book List

A personalized book list is created specific to the courses you are registered for. The system draws on your instructor’s book list for each course. Drawing on your enrollment record, an automated process links to the book titles. View your personal book list on-line when you login at the Online Self-Service System.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I view my Personalized Book List?

Select [Student Menu], [Student Accounts], and then select [View Personalized Book List].

Q: When can I view my Personalized Book List?

The first date to view my Personalized Book List is generally two weeks after the first date of registration. When you have registered for a course, the program accesses your enrollment record and links to just those books used in your courses.

Q: How does the system know what books I’ll need?

The system draws on your instructor’s book List for each course. Drawing on your enrollment record, an automated process links to the book titles. You may print this record (Personalized Book List) at anytime.

Q: Is there a full book list I can refer to?

A full list of textbooks for the semester will be displayed on the Book List page.
